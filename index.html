Save Dat Monie

Save Dat Monie is a simple chrome extension that reminds you of your goal to save more money by spending lesser of it. Every time you visit a website that wants you to buy something or pay for something, Save Dat Monie will show a pop-up. It was developed to curb a close friend's habit of spending insane amounts of money on Amazon if he wasn't told to stop or reminded to save. Install the extension to see how it works! And if you're a compulsive Amazon shopper like my friend, I can assure you that you'll save some money!

Movie Recommender

Built a Movie Recommender that asks you to rate some movies and then recommends movies tailored to your taste. This is done by comparing your movies and ratings to that of other raters to obtain your similarity rating with each rater in our database. It then looks at the top-rated movies by k raters who are most similar to you. The top h movies so obtained are then recommended to you. It also has the ability to make recommendations after filtering movies by Directors, Year of Release, Genre, etc.
